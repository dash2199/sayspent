Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

SaySpent is designed with privacy in mind.

All voice recordings and expense data stay on your device. We do not store, transmit, or access your personal financial information.

Voice Data

  • On-Device Processing: Voice input is processed using Apple's Speech Recognition framework.
  • No Voice Storage: Voice recordings are not stored or transmitted to external servers.
  • Real-Time Conversion: Speech is converted to text immediately and audio is discarded.
  • User Control: You may deny microphone permission. Manual expense entry will continue to work.

Expense Data

  • Local Storage Only: All expense data is stored exclusively on your device.
  • System Protection: Data is protected by iOS security and encryption mechanisms.
  • No Cloud Sync: SaySpent does not sync or back up data to external servers.
  • Full Control: You may delete any or all data at any time within the app.

Data Collection

SaySpent does not collect personal data automatically and does not use third-party analytics or tracking tools.

In-App Purchases

Payment for SaySpent Pro subscriptions is processed by Apple. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment card details. Apple's privacy policy applies to purchase transactions: apple.com/legal/privacy.
