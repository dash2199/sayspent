Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

All your voice recordings and expense data stay on your device. We don't store, transmit, or access your personal financial information.

Voice Data

  • Local Processing: Voice recordings are processed entirely on your device using Apple's Speech Recognition
  • No Storage: We do NOT store or transmit your voice recordings
  • Real-time Conversion: Speech is converted to text immediately and the audio is discarded
  • Your Control: You can deny microphone permission - manual entry still works

Expense Data

  • Local Storage: All expense data is stored only on your device
  • iOS Encryption: Protected by Apple's built-in encryption
  • No Cloud Sync: Currently, we don't sync data to external servers
  • Full Control: Delete any or all expenses anytime from Settings
